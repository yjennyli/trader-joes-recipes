Ultimate Fig Burrata Salad

405+ cal/serving $9.99+

A luxurious salad combining creamy burrata, sweet figs, and peppery arugula with a balsamic drizzle.

Directions:

  1. Wash and dry the arugula, then arrange on a large serving platter.
  2. Tear the burrata into pieces and scatter over the arugula bed.
  3. Slice the dried figs and arrange them around the burrata.
  4. Roughly chop the macadamias and sprinkle over the salad.
  5. Drape slices of prosciutto artfully across the top.
  6. Drizzle generously with extra virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  7. Season with salt and freshly cracked black pepper to taste.
  8. Serve immediately while the burrata is fresh and creamy.

Key Ingredients:

  • Greek Yogurt
  • Burrata Cheese
  • Macadamias
  • Dried Fig Slices
  • Organics Arugula
  • Sliced Prosciutto
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Balsamic Vinegar

Chestnut Chicken Mushroom Risotto

480+ cal/serving $5.45+

A hearty, umami-rich risotto featuring tender chicken, earthy mushrooms, and sweet chestnuts.

Directions:

  1. Season chicken thighs with salt and pepper. Sear in a hot pan until golden, then set aside.
  2. In the same pan, sauté shiitake and baby bella mushrooms until browned and fragrant.
  3. Add minced ginger, diced carrots, and scallion whites. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add rice and toast for 1 minute, stirring constantly.
  5. Stir in the condensed mushroom soup and gradually add warm broth.
  6. Sprinkle in the umami seasoning blend and stir to combine.
  7. Slice the seared chicken and chestnuts, fold into the risotto.
  8. Garnish with scallion greens and serve hot.

Key Ingredients:

  • Steamed Chestnuts
  • Shiitake Mushrooms
  • Baby Bella Mushrooms
  • Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • Umami Seasoning
  • Chicken Thighs
  • Carrots
  • Scallions
  • Ginger
  • Rice
