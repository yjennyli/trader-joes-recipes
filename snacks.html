Pumpkin Taro Cream Soup

240 cal/serving $4.74+

Smooth and creamy pumpkin milk soup with bouncy taro balls, a taste of fall.

Directions:

  1. Cook frozen taro balls according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, combine organic pumpkin puree with milk over medium heat.
  3. Add cream and sugar, stirring until well combined and heated through.
  4. Use an immersion blender to make the soup extra smooth (optional).
  5. Pour the warm soup into a serving bowl.
  6. Add the cooked taro balls on top.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy the cozy flavors!

Key Ingredients:

  • Organic Pumpkin Can 200g
  • Frozen Taro Balls 50g
  • Organic Milk A2/A2 75g
  • Cream 10g
  • Sugar 1g

Assam Milk Tea Float

350 cal/serving $2.83

Much richer than regular black tea. A creamy, indulgent treat!

Directions:

  1. Boil water and steep 3 Assam black tea bags for 4-5 minutes for a strong brew.
  2. Remove tea bags and let the tea cool slightly.
  3. Pour the warm tea into a tall glass, filling about 2/3 full.
  4. Add organic milk to your desired creaminess.
  5. Top with a generous scoop of French vanilla ice cream.
  6. Watch it float and slowly melt into the tea!
  7. Serve immediately with a long spoon and straw.

Key Ingredients:

  • Organic Assam Black Tea 3 bags
  • French Vanilla Ice Cream 50g
  • Organic Milk A2/A2 400ml

Cheese Shizuoka Matcha

450+ cal/serving $4.26+

A fine-dining level dessert drink combining creamy cheese foam with premium matcha.

Directions:

  1. Prepare the coffee panna cotta layer in a clear glass and refrigerate until set.
  2. Whisk matcha powder with a small amount of hot water until smooth.
  3. Heat organic milk and combine with the matcha paste.
  4. Add sweetened condensed milk if desired for extra sweetness.
  5. Pour the matcha mixture over the set panna cotta layer.
  6. Optionally, melt white chocolate wafers and drizzle on top.
  7. Serve chilled for the most refreshing experience!

Key Ingredients:

  • Coffee Panna Cotta 1 serve
  • Organic Milk A2/A2 75g
  • Matcha Green Tea Powder 1 packet
  • Optional: Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • Optional: White Chocolate Wafers

Boba Tea

300 cal/serving $1.76+

Everyone likes it! Make your own boba tea at home with TJ's ingredients.

Directions:

  1. Prepare the boba pearls from the Instant Boba Kit according to package directions.
  2. Place the cooked boba pearls at the bottom of a tall glass.
  3. Add ice cubes to fill the glass halfway.
  4. Pour in the cold brew black tea concentrate.
  5. Add the non-dairy oat creamer with brown sugar flavor.
  6. Top with organic milk.
  7. Stir well with a boba straw and enjoy!

Key Ingredients:

  • Instant Boba Kit 1 pouch
  • Cold Brew Black Tea Concentrate
  • Non-Dairy Oat Creamer Brown Sugar
  • Organic Milk A2/A2 75g
